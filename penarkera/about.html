    WORLD’S LARGEST LIVING HISTORY MUSEUM! "İSTANBUL"

    Welcome to the Müze İstanbul, which has a complete guide to museums and archeological treasures in İstanbul. İstanbul is full of history and culture so it is no wonder some of the best museums in the world are located in Türkiye. We dedicated to provide complete and up-to-date information about İstanbul's historical places and museums. We are gaining ground steadily along the way to take ownership of museums and archaeological sites, which we consider as the most important social responsibility project in Türkiye, and to make and develop museums that live as far as we can. Preserving İstanbul's historical heritage and showcasing the historic legacy of Türkiye are the guiding principles of our directorate. Whether you're looking for art, history or cultural heritage you will find it in İstanbul. Step back in time and discover history of ancient civilizations.
